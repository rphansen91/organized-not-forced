# Derek — The Skeptical Athlete

**Reading:** Prologue (original)  
**Overall Impression:** 6/10 — Interesting but soft

## What Lands

**You had a real stuck point.**
Three months on pike push-ups without progress. That's real. I've been there with overhead pressing — grinding and getting nowhere.

**"I wasn't weak. I was disorganized."**
Okay. This is a different frame. I've always assumed my overhead sucks because my shoulders are jacked up. If you're saying it's a positioning problem, I'm listening.

**The chain concept.**
> "A chain with no leaks."

I get this. In wrestling we called it having a "tight body." When you're organized, force travels through you. When you're not, you leak power everywhere. Same principle.

---

## What Triggers My Bullshit Detector

**"And then something clicked."**
Everyone says this. Show me what changed mechanically.

**"Gravity was doing it for me."**
This sounds like woo. Either explain it or cut it. How is gravity creating scapular depression? I can maybe picture it if I think about pike position — your weight is hanging forward, pulling the scaps down? — but that's me doing the work.

**Surfing.**
I don't surf. This paragraph is for you, not for me. If you'd said "it helped my clean" or "my wrestling changed," I'd care. Surfing feels like a lifestyle brand.

**"Effortless. But strong."**
This is soft language. Give me numbers. Could you do more reps? Add load? Hold longer? "Effortless" isn't a metric.

## Questions I Still Have

1. **What does this mean for loading?** Can I barbell press better if I'm "organized"? Or is this just a bodyweight thing?
2. **How do I know if I'm organized?** What's the test?
3. **Does this actually work for my body?** You're a surfer doing calisthenics. I'm 210 lbs with wrestling wear-and-tear. Different situation.

## Where I Almost Put It Down

"Gravity was doing it for me" without explanation. I almost wrote you off as another movement guy who mystifies basic mechanics.

---

## What Would Make Me Keep Reading

If you connect this to performance. Not feelings. Performance. Did your numbers change? Did your pain change? Show me something objective.

---

## One-Line Verdict

**Interesting principle, but prove it works outside your specific body and your specific sports.**
