# Jordan (The Beginner Dreamer) — Prologue Feedback

**Reading as:** 24-year-old desk worker, can do 8 push-ups and 2 pull-ups, inspired by calisthenics Instagram, not sure if their body can do advanced skills

---

## Overall Reaction

This is cool. I don't fully understand everything he's describing, but it makes me feel like maybe there's hope for me? Like maybe the reason I suck at this isn't that I'm hopeless — maybe I just don't understand something yet.

But I'm also a little intimidated.

---

## What Landed

- **"If you've ever felt like you were fighting your own body—stretching endlessly, grinding through reps, collecting cues that never stick—this book is for you."** — Okay, yes. This is me. I've watched so many YouTube videos and nothing sticks. I feel like I'm doing something wrong but I don't know what.

- **"I wasn't weak. I was disorganized."** — This is actually hopeful? Like maybe my problem isn't that I need to spend years getting strong first. Maybe there's something I can learn that would help now.

- **"Not because I have all the answers. But because I found something that changed everything."** — I like that he's not claiming to be a guru. That makes me trust him more.

---

## What Confused Me

- **"Scapular depression"** — I don't know what this is. I had to guess from context. Is this going to be a book full of anatomy terms I don't know?

- **"Pike position"** — I had to Google this. Wish there was a quick description so I could picture it.

- **"Hollow body"** — Is this a position? A technique? I've heard this term but I don't really know what it means.

- **"Force transfer"** — I think I get it, but could you explain it more simply?

---

## Questions I'm Left With

1. **Can someone like me actually do these skills?** He could do 40 push-ups and 5 pull-ups when he started. That's... a lot? I can do 8 and 2. Am I even the target reader here?

2. **How long did it take him?** He says "the next year" but I don't know what that means exactly. A year to get all 5 skills? Some of them? 

3. **Is there a progression for total beginners?** Or does this book assume I can already do the basics?

4. **What if I try this and nothing clicks for me?** Is the "click" something everyone can find, or did he just get lucky?

---

## Where I Almost Put It Down

**"I could do forty push-ups. Maybe five pull-ups on a good day."** — Honestly, this made me feel like the book might not be for me. That's already pretty strong compared to where I am. If that's the starting point, am I too far behind?

---

## What Would Keep Me Reading

- A clear explanation of where to start if you're a complete beginner
- Definitions or simple explanations of terms (scapular depression, hollow body, etc.)
- Reassurance that this works even if you're not already athletic
- Honest timelines so I know what to expect

---

## Honest Rating

**7/10** — I'm inspired but also a little scared this isn't for me. The message is hopeful, but I need the book to meet me where I am, not assume I'm already halfway there.
