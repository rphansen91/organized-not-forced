# Marcus (The Frustrated Practitioner) — Prologue Feedback

**Reading as:** 32-year-old software engineer, 3 years into calisthenics, stuck on muscle-ups for over a year

---

## Overall Reaction

Okay, this actually got my attention. The opening hooked me because I *know* that feeling of stacking cue on top of cue and getting nowhere. "Depress harder. Squeeze more. Pull your shoulders down." — yeah, that's literally my internal monologue every time I try a muscle-up.

But I have concerns.

---

## What Landed

- **"I'd stacked cue on top of cue, and none of it had worked."** — This is exactly my experience. Finally someone gets it.
- **"Trying harder wasn't working."** — Yes. This is why I picked up the book.
- **The chain description:** "Ground to torso. Torso to scap. Scap to arm. Arm to force." — This is the kind of specific language I want more of.
- **"I wasn't weak. I was disorganized."** — Interesting reframe. I'm skeptical but curious.

---

## What Felt Like Bullshit (or Close to It)

- **"Gravity was doing it for me."** — This sounds poetic but what does it actually mean? Gravity is always there. What changed? I need more than a feeling described.
  
- **"I'd finally gotten out of the way."** — This is the kind of vague advice I've heard a hundred times. "Just relax." "Stop overthinking." Great, HOW?

- **"Something clicked."** — I've read this phrase in every transformation story ever. What specifically clicked? Was it a position? A thought? A sensation? The click is the whole point and you're glossing over it.

---

## Questions I'm Left With

1. **What was the actual difference in your pike position that day?** You describe the feeling but not the setup. Were your hands in a different position? Was your weight distributed differently? I need the mechanical detail.

2. **"40 push-ups, 5 pull-ups"** — That's actually a decent base. Were you strong enough for a muscle-up and just disorganized? Or did you need to get stronger too? This matters for knowing if your insight applies to me.

3. **How long from the pike moment to actually getting the 5 skills?** You mention "the next year" but I want to know the honest timeline for each skill.

4. **Does this work for the muscle-up specifically?** You're describing a pike push-up breakthrough. I need to know if the same principle unlocked the muscle-up or if that's a different chapter.

---

## Where I Almost Put It Down

The surfing paragraph at the end. I don't surf. I don't care about your pop-up. If the whole book is going to connect everything to surfing, I'm out. 

Keep the focus on the skills I came here for.

---

## What Would Keep Me Reading

- More mechanical specificity in the next chapter
- Proof that this isn't just "one weird trick" energy
- Acknowledgment that the journey was hard and took time
- Actual progressions, not just philosophy

---

## Honest Rating

**7/10** — Good hook, genuine voice, but the core insight is still wrapped in vague language. I'll read Chapter 1, but you need to get specific fast or I'll assume this is another "just feel it" book that doesn't actually help.

---

## One Line Summary

*"I'm intrigued but not yet convinced — the feeling you're describing is exactly what I'm chasing, but you haven't told me how to find it yet."*
