## Changes

### 2026-05-26 — Prologue

#### REVERTED: All changes rolled back

**Reason:** Revisions passed persona validation but sacrificed eloquence for explanation. The original prose had rhythm, punch, and mystery. The revisions read like PT notes.

**Examples of what went wrong:**
- Original: "Not a joint. Not a pop. A *connection*." → Revised: "For the first time, my upper body felt like one piece instead of a stack of parts." (Lost the punch)
- Original: "Same ocean. Same board. Different body." → Revised: "Getting out of a chair. Running. Even my surfing pop-up..." (Lost the imagery)
- Added: "Whether you're starting with forty push-ups or four." (Defensive hedging that broke intimacy)

**Lesson learned:** Persona validation is necessary but not sufficient. The feedback loop needs a voice preservation gate. Personas can identify *what's not landing*, but the rewrite must still serve the music of the prose.

**Status:** Original prologue restored. Feedback remains valid — the concerns were real — but the solutions were wrong.
