## Day 2: Film Your First Video (1 hour)

### The Introduction Video
Film yourself talking to camera. Use this script:

```
"I set a goal that most people think is impossible.

By the end of 2026, I'm mastering 5 calisthenics skills:
Pistol squat, muscle-up, handstand push-up, front lever, and full planche.

But here's the thing — I'm not just training.
I'm documenting everything into a book.

Because what I'm learning isn't about trying harder.
It's about understanding how the body actually organizes force.

I'm calling it: Organized, Not Forced.

Follow along. This is going to be wild."
```

**Tips:**
- Natural lighting (face a window)
- Phone at eye level
- Don't overthink it — raw > perfect
- 60-90 seconds max

## The Mindset

**Remember:**
- Raw > perfect
- Consistency > viral moments
- 100 true fans > 10,000 followers
- The journey IS the content
- You're building in public — people love that

**Don't worry about:**
- Perfect lighting
- Professional editing
- Having all the answers
- Growing fast

**Do focus on:**
- Showing up consistently
- Being genuinely helpful in comments
- Sharing real insights and struggles
- Building connections, not just followers
