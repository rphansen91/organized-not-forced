## Platform Priority

### Start With (Pick 1-2):
1. **Instagram** — Best for fitness content, visual focus
2. **TikTok** — Fastest growth potential, younger audience

### Add Later:
3. **YouTube** — Long-form content, searchable
4. **Twitter/X** — Quick thoughts, connect with authors/creators
5. **LinkedIn** — Professional credibility, speaking opportunities

### Don't Bother (Yet):
- Facebook page (unless you have existing network there)
- Pinterest (not your audience)
- Snapchat (not your format)
