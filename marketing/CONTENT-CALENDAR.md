## Content Batching System

### How to Batch Content (1-2 hours/week)

**Step 1: Film during training (15 min extra)**
- Set up phone to record key sets
- Get 3-5 clips per session
- Don't worry about perfection

**Step 2: Weekly content session (1 hour)**
- Review clips from the week
- Pick best 3-4 for posts
- Write captions using templates
- Schedule for the week

**Step 3: Daily engagement (10-15 min)**
- Respond to comments
- Engage with other accounts
- Post Stories as needed
