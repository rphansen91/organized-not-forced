## KEY PRINCIPLE
**"It's not about pulling higher. It's about pulling *through*."**

The muscle-up isn't a high pull-up. It's a different motor pattern entirely:
- Pull → Turn → Press (not Pull → Stop → Lower)
- Explosive row into dip (not vertical pull)
- Hollow body stays connected (not core disconnect at peak)
